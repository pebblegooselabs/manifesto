What We Do

Two pillars.
One methodology.

01
Financial Fraud & Accountability
We trace money through IRS 990 filings, FEC campaign finance data, state lobbying registries, corporate filings, and FOIA responses. We map shell company networks, document grant fraud, and surface undisclosed lobbying coordination. If it moved through the financial system and left a public record, we will find it.
02
Environmental Pollution & Enforcement Gaps
We document permit violations, illegal discharge incidents, contamination events, and regulatory failures using EPA ECHO, state environmental databases, satellite imagery, and geospatial analysis. We do not take positions on policy. We document what happened, whether enforcement followed, and who bears the burden.

About

What DIOMEDES is.

DIOMEDES RESEARCH is an independent public interest research organization based in Florida. We investigate financial fraud and environmental pollution using open-source methodology and publicly available records — and publish everything freely.

We are not a journalism outlet. We are not a law firm. We are not an advocacy organization. We are investigative infrastructure — the connective tissue between raw public records and the communities, lawyers, journalists, and regulators who need them.

All tools are released under AGPL licensing. All findings are published openly. No one pays us to find out what their government is doing. That is the point.

DIOMEDES does not advocate. DIOMEDES does not lobby. DIOMEDES does not tell anyone what to do with what it finds. DIOMEDES follows the records. Regulators, courts, and the public decide what comes next.
